Branding

Branding is more than adding your logo to the application. Succesfull branding involves branding color, feel and other assets. For us the look and feel is just as important as the functionality of the applcation. Keep in mind that the user needs a memorable experience for the replay value of an applcation.

Using your logo inside the sidebar

For most application the color and font usage will fill in your branding. Try to resist using your logo on every page. Make sure you give your header or logo enough spacing inside the bar.
