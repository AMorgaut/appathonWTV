Layout

With your layout, you show users what's most important, what their choices are, and how things are related. Depending on the option to go - Fullscreen or Sidebar - your layout may vary.

Make sure you give interactive elements enough spacing, a high contrast and a large enough size to make it easy for the user to interact with the content and controls. Use a font-size of atleast - 24 pixels / 18 points and a minimum icon-size of - 75px by 75px.

Important note: the distance between the user and the User Interface is further than the average distance on a computer or smartphone.

Sidebar

The sidebar view is mostly used for - side by side - and - contextual applications. The useable area is completely free to fill in for the designer and/or developer.

Important note: keep in mind that (most of) the functionality in the header and footer can't be changed.

Useable area 980 pixels by 600 pixels

Fullscreen

Mostly used for applications that needs the users complete attention, for example games and applications like Netflix. It's possible to mute the sounds of the television channel or show it on top of the fullscreen application.

Important note: although you can use the edge of the screen for backgrounds, it's not recommended to put application functionality in this area.

Useable area 1880 pixels by 980 pixels
