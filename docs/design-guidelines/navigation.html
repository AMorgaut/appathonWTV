Navigation

Most of the time a user does not notice a good navigation, but as soon as it doesn't meet their expactations it gets the stamp of a bad navigation. Its the developers job to set up a good navigation and structure. In MAF the developer is free to implement any type of menu. This freedom comes with a responsibility.

Focus

The focus color is important for the navigation trough the apllication and the menu. Besides the active color (blue), the developer needs to choose a focus color (red in the example).
